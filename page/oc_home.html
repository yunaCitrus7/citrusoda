Musubi

  • Octoling Girl
  • Age: 21
  • Height: 168 cm
  • Job: Hacker

Her personality is strong and assertive — cheerful, lively, clingy, playful, and a bit unpredictable.
A former Octarian squad leader, she hacked into the system after coming to the surface to erase her own records and officially leave the military.
One night, after getting drunk, she collapsed on the roadside and was rescued by a kind surface boy. From their first meeting, she felt a strange sense of attachment to him — claiming that even without knowing his name, she could tell he was “her type.” She later moved in to start a shared life with him. Over time, their feelings deepened, and she eventually confessed and started dating him.
She has an older brother named Shuu (終) whom she’s very close to. The siblings have a strong emotional bond. However, due to temporarily losing contact after both arrived on the surface, she once resented him deeply.

Yuuhi

  • Inking Boy
  • Age: 19
  • Height: 175 cm
  • Job: Grizzco Worker

Gentle personality, lacks confidence.
An only child, Yuuhi lost his mother to illness when he was just 2 years old. His father later fell into gambling, leaving the family burdened with massive debt.
At the age of 14, his father disappeared without a word, and the debt collectors came knocking. From that point on, Yuuhi began a life of non-stop part-time work to pay off what was left behind.
Due to long-term overwork and lack of sleep, he often has dark circles under his eyes. Outside of work, he tends to be slow and a little spacey — but he's extremely skilled in Salmon Run.
One day after work, he encountered Musubi passed out drunk on the street. Worried about her, he took the unconscious girl home and cared for her. That’s how they met.
As they began living together, feelings slowly grew between them. After a sudden moment of realization, he finally understood his emotions and confessed to Musubi, beginning their relationship.

Shuu

  • Octoling Boy
  • Age: 23
  • Height: 188 cm
  • Job: Archary Player

He always gives off a friendly and laid-back impression with his easy smile — though in truth, the only ones he truly relaxes around are Musubi and Yuuhi.
A former Octarian squad commander, Shuu cut ties with the military after arriving on the surface, believing he could live more freely this way. Since then, he’s made a living through Ranked Battles, relying on his exceptional combat ability to win prizes and earnings.
He has great confidence in his appearance and knows how to use that charm to his advantage, having built a wide network of strong allies and connections on the surface.
Upon learning that his younger sister Musubi had also made it to the surface, he quickly sought her out. Reunited, the siblings resumed their close bond — and, as fate would have it, found something new: a gentle happiness shared with Yuuhi, beginning a warm and unshakable life together.
